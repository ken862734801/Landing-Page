Who is Naval Ravikant?

Considered one of the greatest thinkers of our generation, Naval Ravikant is an angel investor, podcaster, and entrepreneur. He is the cofounder of AngelList and the founder of several other technology companies such as Epinions, Vast.com, MetaStable, and Genoa Corp.
