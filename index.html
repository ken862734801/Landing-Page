Who is Naval Ravikant?

Considered one of the greatest thinkers of our generation, Naval Ravikant is an angel investor, podcaster, and entrepreneur. He is co-founder of AngelList and the founder of several technology companies such as Epinions, Vast.com, MetaStable, and Genoa Corp.
